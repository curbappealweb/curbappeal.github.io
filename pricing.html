FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to have any content ready before we start? +
Not necessarily! We'll guide you through what we need — like photos, your business info, and the main message you want to send. If you have content ready, great. If not, we can help you figure out what to include.
What if I already have a website and just need it improved? +
We do redesigns all the time! We'll evaluate your current site and give you an honest assessment of what needs fixing. Redesign pricing is typically the same as new builds.
Can I update my own website after it's built? +
Yes! We can build your site on a simple content management system (CMS) so you can make basic updates yourself. Or, with a maintenance plan, we handle all updates for you — whichever you prefer.
What does the monthly maintenance fee cover? +
Monthly hosting, security monitoring, software updates, regular backups, and up to 1 hour of content updates per month (text/image changes). It keeps your site safe, fast, and up to date.
How long does it actually take to build a website? +
Launchpad sites typically take about 1 week. Growth sites take 2 weeks. Flagship sites take 3–4 weeks. The biggest factor is how quickly you provide feedback and content — the faster you respond, the faster we deliver!
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We typically ask for 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can discuss a 3-payment schedule. We want to make it easy for local businesses to get the help they need.
